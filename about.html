About This Project

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of electricity consumption patterns across Indian states during a critical period that includes the COVID-19 pandemic and nationwide lockdown.

Project Objective

To analyze and understand electricity consumption patterns across Indian states from January 2019 to December 2020, with special focus on:

  • Overall consumption trends and year-over-year changes
  • Regional variations in electricity demand
  • Impact of COVID-19 lockdown on energy usage
  • Post-lockdown recovery patterns across states

Project Scenarios

Scenario 1
Change in Overall Consumption Trends

From January 2019 to December 2020, India's electricity consumption underwent significant changes influenced by seasonal demand variations, economic growth fluctuations, and COVID-19 lockdown impact.

Scenario 2
Regional Variations in Demand

Electricity consumption did not follow the same pattern across all regions of India. Northern, Southern, Eastern, Western, and Northeastern states experienced differing usage levels.

Scenario 3
Recovery After Lockdown

Following COVID-19 restrictions, electricity demand began to recover, but the pace of recovery differed widely across states.
